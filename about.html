ABOUT US

We specializes in making of bags, footwears and productions of garri(cassava flour)

WHO ARE WE

My business started in the year 2017, when i was still an undergraduate, i noticed the garri(cassava flour) that was being sold to us in school were not neat enough, so i decided to make and sell a well prepared and presentable garri to my colleagues.After graduation from university, i decided to learn a skill which is bag making and footwears

PASSIONS

I have always been passionate about learning a skills, which people can benefit from and in returns i will be able to make profit from.So i decided to learn bag making and footwears and also produced a well packaged garri that is free from dirts.

VISIONS

Our focus on the future to be a leading producer & manufacturer globally, we strive to provide solution that would grow together the organization and our clients.To be a responsible corporate citizen to our society.

GOALS

To innovate, to lead, to enhance, to provide best-value products and service to globals customers and to make a diffrence through our branding to stay ahead of fashion trends and latest technology.

WHY CHOOSE US
